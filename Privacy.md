# ForgeGym Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: January 2024

ForgeGym is built with absolute privacy as a core engineering requirement. We believe your health and training data should remain private and under your exclusive control.

## 1. Data Collection
ForgeGym does **not** collect any personal data.
*   **No Accounts**: You do not need to sign up or provide an email address.
*   **No Tracking**: We do not use any third-party analytics (like Google Analytics or Firebase) to track your usage.
*   **No Cloud Sync**: We do not upload your workout history to any servers.

## 2. Data Storage
All information you enter (workouts, sets, body measurements) is stored **locally on your device** in a private SQLite database managed by the app.

## 3. Data Ownership
You have 100% ownership of your data.
*   **Backup**: You can export your entire history as a JSON file at any time.
*   **CSV Export**: You can export your training logs to a standard CSV format for use in Excel or Google Sheets.
*   **Deletion**: You can wipe all data instantly from the Settings menu.

## 4. Third-Party Services
ForgeGym does not integrate with any third-party advertising or marketing services.

## 5. Contact
If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out via our GitHub repository.
